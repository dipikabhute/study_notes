## 1️⃣ Q. Explain Patents and its Objectives.

### Answer:

#### 📌 Patent

A patent is a legal right granted to an inventor to protect their invention for a limited period.

### 🎯 Objectives of Patents

• Encourage innovation
• Protect inventors' rights
• Promote research and development
• Prevent unauthorized use of invention
• Provide economic benefit to inventors
• Promote technological advancement
---

## 2️⃣ Q. Explain Patent Ownership and Rights.

### Answer:

### 👤 Patent Ownership

Patent ownership refers to the person or organization that owns the invention.

Patent Owner Can Be

• Individual inventor
• Company / organization
• Employer (employee invention)
• Joint inventors

### 📌 Rights of Patent Owner

• Right to manufacture invention
• Right to sell invention
• Right to license invention
• Right to transfer patent
• Right to stop unauthorized use
---


## 3️⃣ Q. Explain Patent Assignment with Types.

### Answer:

### 📄 Patent Assignment

Patent assignment means transfer of ownership of patent from one person to another.

### Types of Assignment

• Full assignment — Complete transfer of rights
• Partial assignment — Limited transfer
• Assignment with goodwill
• Assignment without goodwill

### Requirements

• Written agreement
• Signature of parties
• Patent details
• Date of assignment
---


## 4️⃣ Q. Explain Defences in case of Patent Infringement.

### Answer:

### 🛡 Defences in Patent Infringement

• Patent is invalid
• No infringement occurred
• Prior use defence
• Experimental use
• Independent invention
• Expired patent

These defences help the accused person avoid legal liability.
---

## 5️⃣ Q. Explain Patent Enforcement.

### Answer:

Patent enforcement refers to legal actions taken by patent owner to protect rights.

### ⚖ Enforcement Methods

• Filing lawsuit
• Court injunction
• Claim damages
• Stop production
• Seizure of products
---


## 6️⃣ Q. Explain Patent Infringement and Remedies.

### Answer:

⚠️ Patent Infringement

Patent infringement occurs when someone uses patented invention without permission.

### Types of Infringement

• Direct infringement
• Indirect infringement
• Contributory infringement

Remedies

• Injunction
• Damages
• Seizure of goods
• Compensation
---

## 7️⃣ Q. Explain Patent Rights and Ownership in Detail.

### Answer:

### 📌 Patent Rights

• Exclusive rights to invention
• Right to sell invention
• Right to license
• Right to transfer

### 👤 Ownership

• Inventor
• Employer
• Company
• Joint owners

Patent ownership gives complete control over invention.
