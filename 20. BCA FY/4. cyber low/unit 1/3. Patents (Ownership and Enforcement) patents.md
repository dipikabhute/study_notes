1️⃣ Patents
🎯 Objectives of Patents

• Encourage innovation
• Protect inventors' rights
• Promote research and development
• Prevent unauthorized use of inventions
• Provide economic benefits to inventors
---

2️⃣ Patent Ownership

Patent ownership means who owns the invention.

👤 Patent Owner Can Be:

• Individual inventor
• Company / organization
• Employer (in case of employee invention)
• Joint inventors

📌 Rights of Patent Owner

• Exclusive right to use invention
• Right to sell invention
• Right to license invention
• Right to transfer patent
• Right to stop unauthorized use
---

3️⃣ Patent Rights

Patent holder gets:

• Manufacturing rights
• Selling rights
• Distribution rights
• Import rights
• Licensing rights

⏳ Duration of Patent

• Generally 20 years from filing date
• After expiry → Public domain
---

4️⃣ Patent Assignment

Patent assignment means transfer of ownership.

📌 Types of Assignment

• Full Assignment → Complete transfer
• Partial Assignment → Limited rights transfer
• Assignment with goodwill
• Assignment without goodwill

📄 Assignment Must Include

• Written agreement
• Signature of parties
• Details of invention
• Date of transfer
---

5️⃣ Patent Infringement

Patent infringement means unauthorized use of patented invention.

⚠️ Types of Infringement

• Direct infringement
• Indirect infringement
• Contributory infringement

📌 Examples

• Copying patented product
• Selling patented invention without permission
• Manufacturing patented product
---

6️⃣ Defences in Case of Infringement
🛡 Common Defences

• Patent is invalid
• No infringement occurred
• Experimental use
• Prior use defence
• Independent invention
• Expired patent
---

7️⃣ Remedies for Patent Infringement

Patent owner can take legal action:

• Injunction (Stop usage)
• Damages (Compensation)
• Seizure of products
• Court orders

🌍 International Patent Organizations

• World Intellectual Property Organization
• World Trade Organization
