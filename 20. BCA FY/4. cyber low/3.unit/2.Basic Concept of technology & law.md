# 1. Basic Concepts of Technology and Law
Introduction to Cyber Law

Cyber Law refers to laws that deal with computers, internet, digital communication, and online activities.

## Cyber law is also known as:

✔ Internet Law
✔ IT Law
✔ Digital Law
✔ Cyber Space Law

### Objectives of Cyber Law

✔ Prevent cyber crimes
✔ Protect digital transactions
✔ Protect user privacy
✔ Secure electronic communication
✔ Legal recognition to digital signatures

# 2. Understanding the Technology of Internet

### What is Internet?


The Internet is a global network of computers connected together to share information.

## Features of Internet

✔ Global connectivity
✔ Fast communication
✔ Information sharing
✔ Online services

## How Internet Works

### The Internet works using:

✔ Client
✔ Server
✔ ISP (Internet Service Provider)
✔ Protocols

## Components of Internet

### 1. Client

User's computer requesting information

### 2. Server

Computer providing information

### 3. ISP

Provides internet connection

### 4. Protocols

Rules of communication

## Examples:

✔ HTTP
✔ FTP
✔ TCP/IP

# 11. The Science of Cryptography

## Meaning:


Cryptography is technique to secure information using encryption

### Objectives of Cryptography

✔ Confidentiality
✔ Integrity
✔ Authentication
✔ Security

## Types of Cryptography

### 1. Symmetric Key Cryptography

✔ Same key used for encryption and decryption

### Example:

Password encryption

### 2. Asymmetric Key Cryptography

✔ Two keys used

✔ Public key
✔ Private key

## Encryption Process

Plain Text → Encryption → Cipher Text

## Decryption Process

Cipher Text → Decryption → Plain Text

## Advantages of Cryptography

✔ Secure communication
✔ Protect data
✔ Prevent hacking
