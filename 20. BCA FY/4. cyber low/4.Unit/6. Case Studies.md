# Q1. Explain Case Study of Bank Account Hacking

##Answer:

### Case

A hacker gained unauthorized access to a customer's online bank account and transferred ₹50,000 to another account.

### Cyber Crime

✔ Hacking
✔ Unauthorized access
✔ Data theft

### Applicable Law

✔ Section 66 – Hacking of Computer System
✔ Section 43 – Damage to Computer

### Punishment

✔ Imprisonment up to 3 years
✔ Fine up to ₹5 lakh
✔ Compensation for damage

# Q2. Explain Case Study of Fake Social Media Profile

## Answer:

### Case

A person created a fake social media account using someone else's photos and posted offensive messages.

### Cyber Crime

✔ Identity theft
✔ Impersonation
✔ Defamation

### Applicable Law

✔ Section 66C – Identity Theft
✔ Section 66D – Cheating by Impersonation

### Punishment

✔ Imprisonment up to 3 years
✔ Fine up to ₹1 lakh

# Q3. Explain Case Study of Publishing Obscene Content

## Answer:

### Case

A person uploaded obscene images of another individual on a website.

### Cyber Crime

✔ Publishing obscene content
✔ Cyber harassment

### Applicable Law

✔ Section 67 – Publishing Obscene Information

### Punishment
First Offence

✔ Imprisonment up to 3 years
✔ Fine up to ₹5 lakh

Second Offence

✔ Imprisonment up to 5 years
✔ Fine up to ₹10 lakh
