# 1. Case Study – Hacking of Bank Account

## Case:

A hacker gained unauthorized access to a customer's online bank account and transferred ₹50,000 to another account.

### Cyber Crime

✔ Hacking
✔ Unauthorized access
✔ Data theft

### Applicable Law

✔ Section 66 — Hacking of Computer System
✔ Section 43 — Damage to computer system

### Punishment

✔ Imprisonment up to 3 years
✔ Fine up to ₹5 lakh
✔ Compensation for damage

# 2. Case Study – Fake Social Media Profile

## Case:

A person created a fake Facebook account using someone else's photos and posted offensive messages.

### Cyber Crime

✔ Identity theft
✔ Impersonation
✔ Defamation

### Applicable Law

✔ Section 66C — Identity theft
✔ Section 66D — Cheating by impersonation

### Punishment

✔ Imprisonment up to 3 years
✔ Fine up to ₹1 lakh

# 3. Case Study – Publishing Obscene Content

## Case:

A person uploaded obscene images of another individual on a website.

### Cyber Crime

✔ Publishing obscene content
✔ Cyber harassment

### Applicable Law

✔ Section 67 — Publishing obscene information

### Punishment

First Offence

✔ Imprisonment up to 3 years
✔ Fine up to ₹5 lakh

Second Offence

✔ Imprisonment up to 5 years
✔ Fine up to ₹10 lakh
