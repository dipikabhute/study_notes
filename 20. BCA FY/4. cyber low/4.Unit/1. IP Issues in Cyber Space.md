# 1. Intellectual Property Issues in Cyber Space

## Introduction

Intellectual Property (IP) refers to creations of mind such as:

✔ Software
✔ Music
✔ Videos
✔ Digital content
✔ Websites

In cyberspace, these works can be copied and distributed easily, leading to intellectual property issues.

# 2. Copyright in Digital Media

## Meaning

Copyright protects original works such as:

✔ Software
✔ Music
✔ Movies
✔ E-books
✔ Websites

## Copyright Issues in Digital Media

### 1. Illegal Downloading

✔ Downloading pirated movies
✔ Downloading paid software illegally

### 2. Software Piracy

✔ Unauthorized copying of software
✔ Sharing cracked software

### 3. Digital Copying

✔ Copying content from websites
✔ Copying images without permission

### 4. File Sharing

✔ Sharing copyrighted material online
✔ Uploading pirated content

## Copyright Protection Methods

✔ Digital Rights Management (DRM)
✔ Watermarking
✔ Encryption
✔ Licensing

# 4. Rights of Netizens

## Meaning:

Netizens are internet users.

They have certain rights in cyberspace.

### Rights of Netizens

✔ Right to privacy
✔ Right to freedom of expression
✔ Right to access information
✔ Right to security

# 6. E-Governance

## Meaning

E-Governance refers to use of internet by government to provide services.

### Objectives of E-Governance

✔ Improve efficiency
✔ Transparency
✔ Faster services
✔ Reduce corruption

## Types of E-Governance

1. G2C (Government to Citizen)

### Example:

✔ Online bill payment
✔ Online certificates

2. G2B (Government to Business)

✔ Tax filing
✔ Licenses

3. G2G (Government to Government)

✔ Data sharing between departments

4. G2E (Government to Employee)

✔ Online salary
✔ Employee services

### Types of Cyber Crimes

### Objectives:

# 8. Ethical Hacking

## Meaning:

Ethical hacking is legal hacking done to improve security.

### Objectives of Ethical Hacking

✔ Find security loopholes
✔ Prevent cyber attacks
✔ Improve system security

## Types of Ethical Hackers

1. White Hat Hacker

✔ Legal hacker
✔ Works for security

2. Black Hat Hacker

✔ Illegal hacker

3. Grey Hat Hacker

✔ Between legal and illegal

## Advantages of Ethical Hacking

✔ Improve security
✔ Prevent data theft
✔ Protect systems
