# Q1. What is Copyright in Digital Media?

## Answer:

Copyright in digital media refers to legal protection given to digital content such as software, music, videos, e-books, and websites. It prevents unauthorized copying, distribution, and use of digital content.

# Q2. What is Patent in Cyber World?

## Answer:

A patent in the cyber world protects new inventions related to software, internet technologies, and digital systems. It gives exclusive rights to the inventor.

# Q3. Who are Netizens?

## Answer:

Netizens are individuals who actively use the internet for communication, information sharing, and online services.

# Q4. What is E-Governance?

## Answer:

E-Governance refers to the use of internet and digital technologies by the government to provide services to citizens.

# Q5. What is Ethical Hacking?

## Answer:

Ethical hacking is legal hacking performed to identify security weaknesses and improve system security.

# Q6. What is Cyber Crime?

## Answer:

Cyber crime refers to illegal activities carried out using computers, internet, or digital devices.
 
# Q7. What is Privacy in Cyber World?

## Answer:

Privacy in cyber world refers to protection of personal information from unauthorized access.

# Q8. What is Freedom of Expression?

## Answer:

Freedom of expression is the right to express opinions and ideas freely on the internet.

# Q13. Explain E-Governance

## Answer:

E-Governance refers to use of digital technology by government.

### Objectives

✔ Transparency
✔ Efficiency
✔ Faster services
✔ Reduce corruption

### Types of E-Governance

1. G2C (Government to Citizen)

✔ Online bill payment
✔ Certificates

2. G2B (Government to Business)

✔ Tax filing
✔ Licenses

3. G2G (Government to Government)

✔ Data sharing

4. G2E (Government to Employee)

✔ Employee services
