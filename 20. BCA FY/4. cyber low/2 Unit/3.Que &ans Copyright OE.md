## 1️⃣ What is Copyright?

### Answer:

Copyright is a legal right given to the creator of original work to protect their creation from unauthorized use.

Under Copyright Act, 1957, copyright protects:

• Books 📚
• Software 💻
• Music 🎵
• Films 🎬
• Websites 🌐
• Images 🖼️

### Purpose

• Protect creator
• Prevent copying
• Encourage creativity

## 2️⃣ Explain Objectives of Copyright

### Answer:

The objectives of copyright are:

### 🎯 Objectives

• ✔️ Protect original work
• ✔️ Encourage creativity
• ✔️ Provide financial reward
• ✔️ Promote innovation
• ✔️ Prevent misuse

### Example

Software developer creates app 💻
Copyright protects it from copying.

## 3️⃣ Explain Rights of Copyright Owner

### Answer:

Copyright owner has exclusive rights:

### 🔐 Rights

• ✔️ Right to reproduce
• ✔️ Right to publish
• ✔️ Right to distribute
• ✔️ Right to translate
• ✔️ Right to adapt
• ✔️ Right to perform publicly

### Example

Author writes book 📚
Only author can publish or sell.

## 4️⃣ Explain Transfer of Copyright

### Answer:

Transfer of copyright means giving rights to another person.

### Types of Transfer
### 1. Assignment

• Ownership transferred
• Must be written
• Signed agreement

### Example
Author sells book rights.

### 2. License

• Ownership stays with creator
• Permission given

### Example
Software license 💻

## 5️⃣ Explain Work of Employment

### Answer:

Work created by employee during job belongs to employer.

### Features

• Employer owns work
• Employee not owner
• Work created during employment

### Example

Software developer creates program
Company owns software.

## 6️⃣ What is Copyright Infringement?

### Answer:

Copyright infringement means unauthorized use of copyrighted work.

### Examples

• Copying software 💻
• Downloading pirated movies 🎬
• Copying books 📚
• Using images without permission

## 7️⃣ Explain Defences for Copyright Infringement

### Answer:

Defences are legal exceptions.

### 🛡️ Defences

• ✔️ Fair use
• ✔️ Research purpose
• ✔️ Education use
• ✔️ Criticism
• ✔️ News reporting

### Example

Student copying notes for study — Allowed
Selling copied material — Not allowed

## 8️⃣ What is Fair Use?

### Answer:

Fair use allows limited use of copyrighted work without permission.

### Fair Use Includes

• Education
• Research
• Private study
• Criticism
• News reporting

## 9️⃣ Remedies for Copyright Infringement

### Answer:

Legal remedies available:

### ⚖️ Remedies

• ✔️ Injunction
• ✔️ Damages
• ✔️ Profit recovery
• ✔️ Seizure of copies
