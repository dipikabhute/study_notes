## 1️⃣ Copyright – Objectives

Copyright is a legal right given to creators of original works to protect their creations.

### 🎯 Objectives of Copyright

• ✔️ To protect original work
• ✔️ To encourage creativity
• ✔️ To reward creators financially
• ✔️ To prevent unauthorized copying
• ✔️ To promote knowledge sharing

### 📌 Examples of Copyright Work

• Books 📚
• Music 🎵
• Software 💻
• Films 🎬
• Paintings 🎨
• Website content 🌐

## 2️⃣ Rights of Copyright Owner

The copyright owner has exclusive rights:

### 🔐 Copyright Rights

• ✔️ Right to reproduce the work
• ✔️ Right to publish
• ✔️ Right to distribute copies
• ✔️ Right to perform publicly
• ✔️ Right to translate
• ✔️ Right to adapt

### 📌 Example

If someone writes software code 💻
Only the creator can:

• Copy
• Sell
• Modify
• Publish

## 3️⃣ Transfer of Copyright

Copyright can be transferred to another person.

### 🔄 Types of Transfer
1. Assignment

• Ownership transferred completely
• Must be in written form
• Signed by owner

Example:
Author sells book rights to publisher 📚

2. License

• Ownership stays with creator
• Permission given to others

### Example:
Software company gives license to users 💻

## 4️⃣ Work of Employment (Work Made for Hire)

When employee creates work during employment:

✔️ Employer becomes owner
✔️ Employee is not owner

### 📌 Example

• Software developer working in company
• Company owns software

## 5️⃣ Copyright Infringement

Copyright infringement means using copyrighted work without permission.

### 🚫 Examples of Infringement

• Copying software illegally 💻
• Downloading pirated movies 🎬
• Copying book content 📚
• Using images without permission 🖼️

## 6️⃣ Acts Considered as Infringement

• Unauthorized copying
• Public distribution
• Unauthorized translation
• Unauthorized modification
• Uploading pirated content

## 7️⃣ Defences for Copyright Infringement

These are legal exceptions:

### 🛡️ Defences

• ✔️ Fair use
• ✔️ Research purpose
• ✔️ Private study
• ✔️ Criticism or review
• ✔️ News reporting
• ✔️ Educational use

### 📌 Example

✔️ Student copying notes for study — Allowed
❌ Selling copied notes — Not allowed

## 8️⃣ Fair Use

Fair use allows limited use without permission

📚 Fair Use Examples

• Education
• Research
• Criticism
• News reporting

## 9️⃣ Copyright Duration

• Author life + 60 years (India)

## 🔟 Remedies for Copyright Infringement

If infringement occurs:

### ⚖️ Legal Remedies

• ✔️ Injunction
• ✔️ Damages
• ✔️ Account of profit
• ✔️ Seizure of copies
